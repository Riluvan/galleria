From Abu Dhabi

If you are traveling within Abu Dhabi, please follow the road signage to Al Maryah Island. Once on the island, road signage will direct you to The Galleria. The main car park entrance is located on the left as traffic heads north across the island on Hamouda Bin Ali Al Dhaheri St.
